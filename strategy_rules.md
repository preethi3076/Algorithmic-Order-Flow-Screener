# The VWAP Pullback Strategy

**Account Size:** ₹6,800  
**Max Risk Per Trade:** ₹136 Total (Cut at -₹80 MTM on Groww screen due to fees)  
**Timeframe:** 5-minute chart  
**Indicators Needed:** VWAP, Volume Bars  

---

## 1. The Setup (What to look for)
*   **Time Window:** 9:30 AM to 11:00 AM.
*   **The Scanner:** We are looking for stocks that had a massive volume spike in the first 15 minutes but haven't shot up 4-5% yet.
*   **The Trend:** The stock must be trading *above* the VWAP line initially.

## 2. The Trigger (When to get ready)
*   Wait for the stock to pull back (dip) towards the VWAP line.
*   **Volume Check:** As the price drops, the red volume bars must be getting *smaller*. This means sellers are weak and just taking early profits.
*   **The Signal:** The stock touches VWAP, and a bullish reversal candle forms (like a Hammer or Bullish Engulfing).
*   **The Confirmation:** The green volume bar on that reversal candle MUST be taller than the previous red bars. Institutional buyers just stepped in.

## 3. The Execution (How to enter)
*   **Entry:** Buy immediately when the price breaks the high of your green reversal candle.
*   **Stop Loss (Hard Rule):** Place the stop loss exactly 1 tick below the bottom wick of the reversal candle. 
*   **The Math Check:** Calculate your risk. If the distance from your Entry to your Stop Loss means you will lose more than ₹80, **SKIP THE TRADE**. 

## 4. The Exit (How to take profits)
*   **Target 1 (Safe Exit):** When you reach a 1:1.5 Risk-to-Reward ratio, sell 50% of your position. (If you risked ₹80, you are up ₹120).
*   **Target 2 (The Runner):** Move your stop loss to your Entry Price (Break-Even). Let the remaining 50% ride until the stock looks exhausted or you hit 3:15 PM. You now have a risk-free trade.
