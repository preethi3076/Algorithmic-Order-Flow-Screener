# Stock Tracker & Trading Strategy Roadmap

## Phase 1: Education & Foundation
- **Professional Systems**: Learn how pro traders use different systems (trend following, mean reversion) depending on market conditions (trending vs. ranging).
- **Macro & Micro Context**: Understand current economic factors and how they dictate which strategy to use and for how long.
- **Missing Elements**: Learn trading psychology, risk-to-reward ratios, and position sizing.

## Phase 2: Strategy Development
- **The Core Strategy**: Build a simple, effective price action and volume profile strategy.
- **The "Why"**: Understand exactly why this system works, which sectors it fits best, and under what market conditions.
- **Small Capital Mastery**: Create a strict plan for trading intraday with ₹6,800 capital (focusing on high probability setups and extreme risk management).

## Phase 3: Data & Screening System
- **Real-Time Data**: Pull live data from NSE and BSE websites.
- **Live Scanner/Filter**: Build a screener that takes live inputs and suggests stocks based on potential:
  - Intraday
  - Scalping
  - Swing (overnight holds)

## Phase 4: Backtesting & Optimization
- **How to Backtest**: Learn the correct way to manually and algorithmically backtest the strategy using historical real-world data.
- **Efficiency Refinement**: Tweak the rules based on backtesting results to maximize the system's efficiency before risking real capital.
